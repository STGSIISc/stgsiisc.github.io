What's STGS?

We are a bunch of Graduate Students at the Dept. of Mathematics in IISc Bangalore discussing some topics in Topology and Geometry.


The seminar has two modules: Topology and Geometry.


We meet Monday and Friday every week, once for each module.

The meetings are offline, 90 minutes long (approx.) and happen at LH-1 in the Dept Building.

The seminar meetings are pretty informal. As none of us is an expert in the topics presented, there are always a lot of discussions in the meetings.

We aim to understand the hot topics of the field this way and, if possible, come up with some interesting problems and solve them.
