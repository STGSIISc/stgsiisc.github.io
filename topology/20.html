Title Harmonic maps between surfaces and holomorphic quadratic differential (part 2)
Date Speaker Time
24/06/2022 (Friday) Gobinda sau 9:00-10:30 pm
If $u:\mathbb{C}\rightarrow \mathbb{H}^2$ is an orientation preserving harmonic map with polynomial Hopf differential, then the closure of the image of $u$ is an ideal geodesic polygon. We will obtain this result looking geometric behavior of horizontal foliation corresponding to the polynomial Hopf differential.
