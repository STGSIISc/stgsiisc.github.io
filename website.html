Hey there! I think you are here to see how the website is built, right? Well, let's discuss everything one by one, alright!
First of all, this is a non-commercial, non-profitable website hosted on Github, as you can see easily. The exact purpose of it is to record details of different talks given by a group of mathematics students interested in topology and geometry, nothing more than that.
One thing we just want to clarify is that neither we are developers (professional or amateur) nor this website made by developers. Well, then, from where did we get the content of different pages?
At first, we designed what it would look like, then tried to find out different snippet codes for these designs from the internet, and finally merged these codes after several modifications to fit all of them in a format.
Then what about giving credit/attribute/citation? Alright, this page is made to cite all those authors and their works from where we are inspired. So, let's see them one by one.
